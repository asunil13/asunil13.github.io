Research

Molecular Imaging / Magnetic Resonance Technology Lab (MIMRTL)

This research investigates how to integrate AI technology into healthcare through the development of a medical chatbot. Utilizing Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) techniques, the chatbot reads PDFs on a focused topic of case kidney cancer. We explore the effectiveness of RAG techniques in enhancing the chatbot’s response quality. Our research methodology involves developing the chatbot using Python, OpenAI’s GPT 3.5-Turbo, and other LLMs. We evaluate response accuracy using both RAG and non-RAG techniques by creating a large number of questions related to the PDFs and assessing the answers. Our findings demonstrate that RAG improves the chatbot’s ability to provide accurate and informative responses. This study emphasizes AI's potential in healthcare, advancing patient care through innovative chatbot technology.
